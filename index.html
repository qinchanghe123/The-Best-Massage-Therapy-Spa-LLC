FAQs

Helpful details before your visit.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive about 10 minutes early so you have time to check in, choose your service comfortably, and settle into a relaxed state before your appointment begins.

Can I get a massage if I have an injury?

If you have an injury, recent surgery, or ongoing medical concern, please consult your healthcare provider first and let us know before your session so we can help you choose an appropriate service.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please call or text as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. Advance notice helps us keep appointment times available for other guests.

Is there parking available?

Parking availability may vary by time of day. Please call before your visit if you need help planning the easiest arrival option near Merrick Road.
